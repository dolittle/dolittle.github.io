OWIN

Bifrost hooks into the OWIN way of starting up and taking over the main initialization. The BootStrapper in Bifrost uses the WebActivatorEx to hook up the PreApplicationStart and Start methods. With this, the pipeline is configured and Bifrost uses its different conventions to discover and configure your application. The two most important being the ICanConfigure and ICanCreateContainer interfaces. Read more about configuration here and containers here.

Web.config

In order for this mechanism to work, you need to disable the automatic discovery of app startup. This is done by adding the following to your Web.config.

<appSettings>
    <add key="owin:AutomaticAppStartup" value="false" />
</appSettings>
